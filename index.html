ABOUT CASA MARANA

A local bar with a loyal following.

Casa Marana is a locally owned bar in Marana, Arizona, built around three things: great drinks, great pizza, and great people. It’s the place you bring out-of-town friends, celebrate small wins, and turn random weeknights into real nights out.

From the first pour to the last slice, we focus on quality without the attitude — just a welcoming space, a thoughtful menu, and a staff that knows what you like.

DRINKS

Craft beer, cocktails & more.

Whether you’re chasing the newest IPA, a clean lager, a bright sour, or a classic cocktail, our bar is stocked for serious flavor and easy sipping.

Rotating Beer List

A constantly changing lineup of local and regional craft beers, plus standout breweries from beyond Arizona. Ask what’s new — it changes often.

Cocktails Done Right

From classics to house signatures, every cocktail is mixed with fresh ingredients and attention to detail. Zero-proof options keep everyone in the mix.

Beyond Beer

Cider, seltzer, wine, and a thoughtful selection of spirits give you plenty of ways to raise a glass, no matter what you’re into.
