About Casa Marana

Locally owned bar and pizzeria serving Marana with craft beer, cocktails, and pizza made for sharing.

Casa Marana is all about community. Regulars, first-timers, and friends passing through all get the same warm welcome. The space is lively and cozy with a buzz that makes every night feel like the weekend.

Grab a seat at the bar, order a pie, and discover your new favorite tap. Stay for another round—there’s always something fresh pouring.

Craft Beer & Cider

Rotating taps highlight independent breweries and cideries. Ask the crew for recommendations and flights.

Cocktails & Spirits

From classics to house signatures, the bar is stocked for bold flavors, fresh citrus, and late-night favorites.

Neapolitan-Style Pizza

Hand-stretched dough, high-heat bake, and toppings that blend Italian roots with American comfort.
