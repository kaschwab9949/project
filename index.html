ABOUT CASA MARANA

The spot locals recommend first.

Casa Marana is a locally owned bar and pizzeria focused on community, quality, and a vibe that feels like home. Craft beer, cocktails, wine, and pizza all in one place, with a staff that actually remembers your name.

Whether you’re pre‑gaming, watching the game, hosting friends, or just grabbing a drink after work, this is where Marana comes to hang out.
