ABOUT CASA MARANA

A bar built for regulars, but worth the drive.

Casa Marana is a locally owned bar in Marana, AZ, focused on three things: exceptional drinks, memorable nights, and a laid-back atmosphere where everyone actually wants to hang out.

Expect a rotating tap list, serious cocktails without the attitude, and pizza that stands on its own — plus special events, pop-ups, and seasonal takeovers that keep things interesting.

DRINKS

Craft beer, cocktails & more.

From hazy IPAs to clean lagers, barrel-aged sours, and proper classic cocktails, our menu is built for people who care what’s in their glass.

Rotating Beer List

A constantly changing tap list featuring local Arizona breweries and standout breweries from beyond. Ask what’s new — it probably changed this week.

Cocktails Done Right

Classic builds, house signatures, and zero-proof options so everyone has something they actually want to sip.

Beyond Beer

Cider, seltzer, wine & more — plus non-alcoholic options for the designated drivers and anyone taking the night off.

FOOD

Pizza & bar food built to share.

Think artisan-style pizza, shareable small plates, and the kind of food that disappears as soon as it hits the table.

We also partner with rotating food pop-ups and local trucks, so there’s always something new to try.
